Mantech Pavers, The largest manufacturers of interlocking pavers, under the brand name Mantech in Kerala since 2000. The company has strong reputation in the local and regional market delivering high quality pavers and others at competitive prices.

We provide quality tiles and professional installation services with a real focus on customer satisfaction . We combine quality workmanship, superior knowledge and affordable prices to provide you with service unmatched by our competitors. We are the sister concern of AMCON group Ernakulam.

We manufacture different type of pavers, solid and Hollow Bricks, Kerb stones etc. Using advanced machineries

The concrete pavers are individual concrete units manufactured under extremely high pressure vibrating machines. Manufactured to accurate dimensions and quality standard, they continue to be popular because of their case of use, durability and design flexibility. The pavers are proven to the higgh suitable predestine, walkways, residential area, parking lots, side way, park, bus stop,industrial area and even road etc.

Controll of raw materials used including mixing water, cement, aggregates, chemicals, admixtures and coloring pigments.

Controll of curing stage trough continous checking of temperature and humidity of curing chambers.

Testing of randomly chosen from the curing chanber for compressive strength, dimension, density and water absorption. Issue of an internal test certificate if all test tesr criteria are met.
